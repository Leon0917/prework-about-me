Biography of Bob Marley

Iconic Reggae star Bob Marley (born Robert Nesta Marley; February 6, 1945-May 11, 1981) was the most influential Jamaica musician in history,the defining figure of reggae music and spiritual icon and a prophet to maney. His music remains globally popular and his work has strong spiritual and political messages.
